Welcome to Inishowen

Welcome to Inishowen Hot Air Balloon Flights. Enjoy a calm and memorable experience over the beautiful landscapes of Donegal. Flights operate only when weather conditions are safe.

Why Choose Us?

  • Beautiful views of Donegal coastline and countryside
  • Safety-first approach with weather checks
  • Great for special occasions and gift experiences
  • Friendly support from booking to landing

What Happens on the Day?

  1. Arrival and welcome
  2. Safety briefing and weather check
  3. Balloon preparation and take-off
  4. Scenic flight (weather dependent)
  5. Landing and pack-up
