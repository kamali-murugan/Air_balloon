Booking Info

Flight Duration: Around 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Total Experience: Around 2 to 3 hours including preparation and landing.

Best Weather: Light wind, dry weather, and good visibility.

Dress Code: Comfortable clothes, flat shoes, and warm layers.

Health Advice: Not recommended for pregnant travellers or people with serious back problems.

Minimum Age: Children should be at least 7 years old.

Balloon Capacity: Up to 6 people in one balloon.

Note: Final confirmation depends on safe weather conditions.
